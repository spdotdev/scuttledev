Founded in 2024, Scuttle Development represents a dedicated commitment to technical excellence. As a specialized freelance studio, I recognized a gap in the market: businesses often struggle to find a single partner who truly understands both software development and reliable hosting.

Today, I partner directly with clients to provide a holistic, expert approach to their digital presence.
